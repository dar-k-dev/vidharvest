AI Data Processing

Last updated: September 4, 2025

AI Assistant Overview

VidHarvest Pro uses Google's Gemini AI to provide intelligent chat assistance for video downloading.

Data Sanitization

What We Remove Before AI Processing:

  • Video URLs are replaced with [VIDEO_URL] placeholders
  • No conversation history is sent to AI services
  • No user identifiers or session data is transmitted

What Gets Sent to Gemini AI

Only Current Message: We send only your current sanitized message along with our system prompt. No conversation history, URLs, or personal data is transmitted.

Privacy Protections

  • No Memory: AI doesn't remember previous conversations
  • No URLs: Video links never reach the AI service
  • No Tracking: User sessions aren't linked to AI requests

Video Enhancement Processing

Server-Side Processing:

  • Video upscaling, noise reduction, and color correction are processed on our servers using FFmpeg
  • No video content is sent to external AI services
  • Files are automatically deleted after processing

Third-Party AI Services

Google Gemini API:

  • Used for natural language processing of chat responses
  • Only sanitized messages are sent (no URLs or personal data)
  • Subject to Google's API policies

Important: Avoid sharing sensitive personal information in chat messages.

Your Rights

  • Use VidHarvest Pro without the AI chat feature
  • No personal data is stored or transmitted
  • Video content stays on our servers only temporarily

Contact

Questions about AI data processing? Contact us through the app's chat interface.
