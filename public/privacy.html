Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 4, 2025

Data Collection

We collect minimal data:

  • Video URLs you submit for downloading (processed locally, not stored)
  • Download preferences and settings (stored locally in your browser)
  • Chat history (stored locally in your browser)
  • No personal information, accounts, or user profiles

AI Processing

Gemini AI Integration:

  • Chat messages are sent to Google's Gemini AI for processing
  • Video URLs are replaced with placeholders before sending to AI
  • No conversation history is sent to AI services
  • No download data or user information is shared with AI

Data Storage

Local Storage Only:

  • All user data is stored locally in your browser
  • Downloaded files are temporarily stored on our server (deleted within 2 hours)
  • No user accounts or cloud storage of personal data
  • Clear browser data to remove all stored information

Third-Party Services

External Services Used:

  • Google Gemini AI: For chat responses (sanitized messages only)
  • yt-dlp: For video extraction (runs on our servers)
  • FFmpeg: For video processing (runs on our servers)

Cookies & Tracking

No Tracking:

  • No cookies for tracking or analytics
  • No user behavior monitoring
  • No advertising or marketing data collection
  • Only essential browser storage for app functionality

Data Security

Security Measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • Temporary file storage with automatic deletion
  • No permanent storage of user content
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear all local data by clearing browser storage
  • Use the app without providing personal information
  • Request information about data processing
  • Stop using the service at any time

Contact

For privacy questions or concerns, please contact us through the app's chat interface or visit our support page.
